MAY ALLAH PROTECT US QUOTES

This article seeks to provide the best and most comforting ‘May Allah Protect Us’ quotes. It is a reminder to all that only through Allah we seek protection. Without his blessing nothing is possible. We hope this article provides you comfort and helps reaffirm your faith to the greatest religion in the world… Islam.

Allah has the most beautiful names.

He is both Al-Muhaymin and Al-Hafiz, but what exactly does this mean?

These names refer to his attribute of being the supreme protector, the preserver, the guardian, the one who provides security and safeguards.

In the Quran Allah gives the message to Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)

“And when My servants ask you, [O Muhammad], concerning Me – indeed I am near. I respond to the invocation of the supplicant when he calls upon Me. So let them respond to Me [by obedience] and believe in Me that they may be [rightly] guided.”

We may receive some protection from other creations but the real protection comes from Allah. There is no harm to the one who Allah has entrusted.

At times we may grow in frustration or have doubt but trust in Allah’s plan, it may not make sense while you’re living in the moment but only once we go through it we will be able to see the bigger picture.

Allah does not burden a soul beyond that it can bear
2:286

Who can protect you if Allah doesn’t want to protect you? Who can support you if Allah doesn’t want to support you? Protect comes from Allah subhanahu wa ta’ala.

MAY ALLAH PROTECT US DUA:

Recite the following dua in Arabic for protection. Reference is Surah Al-Imran ayat 173:

 حَسْبُنَا ٱللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ ٱلْوَكِيلُ

Transliteration:  hasbunal allahu wa ni’mal wakeel
Translation: Sufficient for us is Allah, and [He is] the best Disposer of affairs.

Humbly requesting for Allah’s help and seeking his forgiveness are among the best duas you can make. Allah loves the pious, the pure, those whose intentions are good. Islam is a religion where your niyyah (intention) matters. It’s good to pray salah and there is a reward in it, but the most important aspect of salah is to do it with your heart.

Do it for the remembrance of Allah, to worship him. It’s not to please your parents or to show how great of a Muslim you are. That is fulfilling your salah to fulfill your ego. Avoid doing this. Pray because you want to, not because you have to.

Another easy, short, yet popular supplication is the Rabbana Atina Fid Dunya dua which you could learn to ask Allah for all the success… in this world and the hereafter.

DUA – MAY ALLAH PROTECT US FROM ILLNESS OR DISEASE

Here is one dua in Arabic from the hadith which can be recited for protection from illness, diseases, or calamity:

 اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالآخِرَةِ اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ فِي دِينِي وَدُنْيَاىَ وَأَهْلِي وَمَالِي اللَّهُمَّ اسْتُرْ عَوْرَاتِي وَآمِنْ رَوْعَاتِي وَاحْفَظْنِي مِنْ بَيْنِ يَدَىَّ وَمِنْ خَلْفِي وَعَنْ يَمِينِي وَعَنْ شِمَالِي وَمِنْ فَوْقِي وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ أَنْ أُغْتَالَ مِنْ تَحْتِي

Transliteration: Allahumma inni as’alukal-‘afwa wal-‘afiyah fid-dunya wal-akhirah. Allahumma inni as’alukal-‘afwa wal-‘afiyah fi dini wa dunyaya wa ahli wa mali. Allahum-mastur ‘awrati, wa amin raw’ati wahfazni min bayni yadayya, wa min khalfi, wa ‘an yamini wa ‘an shimali, wa min fawqi, wa ‘audhu bika an ughtala min tahti

Translation: O Allah, I ask You for forgiveness and well-being in this world and in the Hereafter. O Allah, I ask You for forgiveness and well-being in my religious and my worldly affairs. O Allah, conceal my faults, calm my fears, and protect me from before me and behind me, from my right and my left, and from above me, and I seek refuge in You from being taken unaware from beneath me.

Sahih (Darussalam) – Sunan Ibn Majah 3871

PROTECTION FROM THE HELLFIRE

For Protection from the hellfire. In the Saheeh hadith of An-Nasa’i 5521:

It was narrated that Anas bin Malik said: “The Messenger of Allah [SAW] said: ‘Whoever asks Allah for Paradise three times, Paradise will say: ‘O Allah, admit him to Paradise.’ And whoever seeks protection from Hell three times, Hell will say:

اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْهُ مِنَ النَّارِ

Transliteration: Allahumma Ajirni Min Al-Nar
O Allah, protect him from the Fire.
