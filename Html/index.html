Revealed Knowledge

Qurʾān, (Arabic: “Recitation”) also spelled Quran and Koran, the sacred scripture of Islam. According to conventional Islamic belief, the Qurʾān was revealed by the angel Gabriel to the Prophet Muhammad in the West Arabian towns Mecca and Medina beginning in 610 and ending with Muhammad’s death in 632 CE.

Education

Educate for Life works to ensure integrated provision of education, healthcare and community development services in Atrauli, an extremely deprived and remote rural community in Uttarpradesh in India. Our aim is to enable socio-economic transformation in Atrauli and the surrounding area. Educate for Life works in partnership with local organizations, providing a range of services to our beneficiaries.
